About Crescent Community Radio

Welcome to the online home of Crescent Community Radio, a grassroots, community organisation. We first hit the airwaves in December 1999 regularly broadcasting during Ramadhan and in Milad to celebrate the birthday of the Holy Prophet Muhammad (SAW) but since then, have grown from strength to strength.

Broadcasting in English and other community languages, we respond to our listeners' health and wellbeing and encourage as much participation as possible from all members of the local community, regardless of religious inclination.

We're here to serve as many different communities as possible. Our mission is to provide a platform for voices that might otherwise go unheard, fostering understanding and unity across Rochdale's diverse population.
